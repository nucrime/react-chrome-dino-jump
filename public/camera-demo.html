🦕 Camera Jump Detection Chrome Dino Game

Jump in real life to make the dinosaur jump!

How to Play:

  • 🎥 Camera Permission: Allow camera access when prompted
  • 👤 Position Yourself: Stand in front of your camera so your full body is visible
  • 🎮 Start Game: Click anywhere in the game area to start
  • 🦘 Jump: Jump in real life to make the dinosaur jump over obstacles
  • ⚙️ Adjust Sensitivity: Use the slider in the camera window to adjust jump sensitivity
  • 🔄 Restart: Refresh the page to restart the game
⚠️ Note: This game requires camera access to detect your movements. Your camera feed is processed locally and not transmitted anywhere.
